PAWS

At PAWS we believe that the affection and care that an animal deserves should never be given a price tag. It is our aim to educate as many as possible about the benefits of adopting and providing them with the basic information that they need in order to feel comfortable with this idea. We have provided a platform which makes it as simple as possible to learn about stray and pet care as well as a quick and easy way to register yourself as a potential adopter. This platform acts as a middle man site to help you sign up various shelters without the hassle of physically having to visit each one of them or trying to find information about them online. We have tied up with 20+ shelters around the city (CUPA,Karuna,Maruthy etc.) who will be able to access our form and assess it to confirm your eligibility as an adopter. One can then decide to visit the shelter of their choice and just show them the confirmation mail of their eligibility and pick a pet!
It's as easy as that!!
